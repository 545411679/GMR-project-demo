[ 02 · 反编译三步The Decompile Pass ]

一张妆面 → 三步 → 一份妆教。 One look. Three passes. A tutorial.

这套流程我目前的想法是这样——欢迎挑刺。三步都可独立替换，整个 pipeline 是可重组的。 This is my current take — push back if you see better. Each pass is swappable; the whole pipeline is recomposable.

PASS 01

抽取 · Peel Peel

从素颜 B 与上妆 M 中分离出纯妆面层 D。 From bare B and made-up M, isolate the pure pigment layer D.

D = (M − B·p) / (1−p)

PASS 02

解构 · Decompose Decompose

把 D 拆成 N 个基础颜料色 + 每个颜料在面部的分布图。 Decompose D into N base pigments + each pigment’s spatial map on the face.

用 Porter-Duff 合成把颜料按顺序叠回素颜——每一笔对应你手上的一件事。 Use Porter-Duff compositing to stack pigments back over bare skin — one brushstroke = one thing your hand does.

[ 03 · 分区Region ]

不是给像素上色，
是给嘴唇上色。 Don’t color pixels.
Color lips.

脸先被切成有名字的区域（唇 / 眼 / 颊 / 鼻 / ...），再做颜料解构。妆教因此能说"在嘴唇上点玫瑰色"，而不是"在某团像素上涂颜色"。 The face is first cut into named regions (lips / eyes / cheek / nose / ...) before any color decomposition. That’s why a stroke can say “dab rose on lips”, not “color this blob of pixels”.

[ 05 · 背后的数学The math behind it ]

所有视觉效果，
背后是三个公式。 Everything visual
collapses to three equations.

写在这儿不是为了证明谁的智商——是欢迎被挑战、被改进、被替换。如果你看到更好的解法，我想听。 Not here to flex — here to be challenged, improved, replaced. If you see a better solution, I want to hear it.

EQ / 01

抽取方程 · Peel The Peel Equation

假设上妆图 M 是素颜 B 与妆面颜料 D 的线性混合，p 是裸肤可见比例： Assume the made-up image M is a linear mixture of bare skin B and pigment D, with p the visible bare-skin fraction:

$$ M(x,y) = p \cdot B(x,y) + (1-p) \cdot D(x,y) $$

求解 D： Solving for D:

$$ D(x,y) = \frac{M(x,y) - p \cdot B(x,y)}{1 - p} $$

这是整个项目的地基——一个一阶可解的反问题。 The foundation — a first-order, closed-form inverse.

EQ / 02

凸组合解构 · Convex Decomposition Convex Decomposition

妆面层 D 的每个像素都建模为 N 个基础颜料色 c_k 的凸组合： Each pixel of D is modeled as a convex combination of N base pigments c_k:

$$ D(x,y) \;=\; \sum_{k=1}^{N} \alpha_k(x,y) \cdot c_k, \quad \alpha_k \ge 0,\; \sum_k \alpha_k = 1 $$

调色板 {c_k} 取自像素 RGB 凸包顶点的最远点采样。每像素 α 通过单纯形约束最小二乘求解。 The palette {c_k} comes from farthest-point sampling on the convex-hull vertices of pixel-RGB. Per-pixel α is solved via simplex-constrained least squares.

EQ / 03

Porter-Duff 累积覆盖 · Stroke Compositing Porter-Duff Cumulative Coverage

第 k 笔的可见覆盖率不是 α_k 本身——要除以"该笔及之后所有层的总贡献"，保证最终一笔后精确等于 M： Stroke k’s visible coverage is not α_k itself — it must be divided by “this and all later layers’ total contribution” — so the final stroke equals M exactly:

$$ P_{k+1} \;=\; 1 - (1-p)\cdot\!\!\!\sum_{j=k+1}^{N} \alpha_j(x,y) $$
$$ \lambda_k(x,y) \;=\; \frac{\alpha_k(x,y)\cdot(1-p)}{P_{k+1}} $$
$$ E_k(x,y) \;=\; (1-\lambda_k)\cdot E_{k-1}(x,y) \;+\; \lambda_k \cdot c_k $$

λ 的推导是项目的非平凡贡献——直接用 α 做合成会让最终帧偏离 M。 The λ derivation is the non-trivial part — naively using α drifts the final frame off-target.

K-means → GMM 迁移 K-means → GMM migration

发现 K-means 调色板会让单纯形投影产生块状伪影。已切到 GMM 软后验，明显更平滑。 K-means palette caused blocky artifacts from piecewise-constant simplex projection. Switched to GMM soft posteriors — visibly smoother.

◯ 开放Open

感知损失函数 Perceptual loss

当前 RGB L2 把色相与亮度等权处理。人眼对亮度更敏感——计划迁到 Lab / 加权 RGB。有更好的方案欢迎指点。 Current L2 in RGB weighs hue and brightness equally. The eye is more sensitive to brightness — planning a move to Lab or weighted RGB. Better ideas welcome.

◯ 开放Open

调色板 ≠ 真实颜料 Palette ≠ true pigments

所有像素本身就是混合物，凸包顶点是最极端的混合色，不是品牌方的纯颜料。这是一个会影响商业化的根本约束——我也在想该怎么破。 All pixels are already mixtures; hull vertices are the most extreme mixtures, not real brand pigments. A structural constraint for commercialization — I’m still thinking about how to break it.
